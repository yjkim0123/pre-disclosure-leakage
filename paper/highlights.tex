\section*{Highlights}

\begin{itemize}
\item Pre-disclosure abnormal returns exist but do not predict the direction of actual earnings surprises (49.0\% accuracy, no better than chance).
\item Non-parametric tests (sign test, Wilcoxon) fail to confirm systematic directional drift in pre-disclosure returns.
\item Post-disclosure Day +1 returns respond sharply and correctly to earnings surprises (+1.13 p.p.\ spread, $p$<0.001, 60.6\% direction accuracy).
\item 96.5\% of DART earnings filings occur after market close, making Day 0 effectively pre-disclosure.
\item Results are robust to firm-clustered and month-clustered standard errors.
\end{itemize}
